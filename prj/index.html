About Us

Himchuli Academy is a premier education institution located in Dhuncho pakha. Madhyapur Thimi-7 bhaktapur. Established with a vision to provide quality education. The school is dedicated to academic excellence character development, and holistic student growth.At himchuli academy, we believe in fostering a dynamic learning environment where student are encouraged to think critically, explore their creativity and develop essential life skills. Himchuli Academy takes pride in its vibrant school community where students, teachers and parents work together to create a supportive and inspiring learning atmosphere.
